Welcome to Nexus!

Your ultimate productivity sidebar for Opera GX & Chrome. 14 widgets, 10 themes, and 110+ settings.

1
Click the Nexus icon in your browser toolbar to open the sidebar
2
Press ⋯ More at the bottom of the nav to pin your favorite panels (max 7)
3
Go to ⚙️ Settings to pick your theme, accent color, and 110+ more options
4
Use Ctrl+Shift+S to toggle the sidebar from any page
Tip: Right-click any page to add selections directly to your Todo or Notes
